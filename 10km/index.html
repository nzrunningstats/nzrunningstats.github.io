10km race statistics

The 10km race takes an average of around one hour for a runner to complete. This is equal to a pace of 6 min/km. Refer to the table further down this page if you are interested in any of the 10km race courses.




Effect of course on 10km race completion time. Slower courses have a greater time estimates of $\beta_\text{course}$. To control for the difference between walkers and runners, participants who took more than 80 minutes were discarded from the dataset when building this model.
